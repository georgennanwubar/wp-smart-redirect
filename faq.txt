FAQ - Smart Redirect For Returning Users

Q: What does this plugin do?
A: It redirects returning users based on cookie detection, and supports analytics tracking.

Q: Can I control who gets redirected?
A: Yes, via role-based exclusions and cookie conditions in the settings.

Q: What analytics are supported?
A: Google Analytics (gtag) and server-side GA4 tracking via Measurement Protocol.

Q: What if I don’t use GA?
A: You can disable it from the plugin settings.
